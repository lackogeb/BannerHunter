# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what’s best for the community
- Showing empathy to other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks, trolling, or insulting comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Encouraging or enabling illegal activity

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when representing the project.
